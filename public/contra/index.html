Contra Fan Site

Contra is a run-and-gun video game developed and published by Konami in 1987. It was released for the arcade, and was later ported to various home consoles. Contra is known for its fast-paced action and difficult gameplay.

In Contra, players control two commandos, Bill Rizer and Lance Bean, as they fight through a series of levels to defeat the Red Falcon Organization, a terrorist group led by an alien warlord known as the Emperor. Players can use a variety of weapons, including machine guns, rocket launchers, and grenades.

Contra was a critical and commercial success, and is considered to be one of the most influential run-and-gun video games of all time. It spawned a popular franchise that includes sequels, prequels, and spin-offs.
